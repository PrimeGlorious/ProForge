About us

ProForge is a job platform built around focus and simplicity. No distractions, no noise — just clear listings, real companies, and a direct path between them and professionals.

We’re not here to gamify hiring or turn it into a circus. There’s no points, no badges, no endless steps — just jobs and people who want to work.

We don’t serve ads. We don’t sell profiles. ProForge is independent, product-driven, and not built for exit strategies — it’s built to be used.

Launched in 2025. No buzzwords. Just the essentials.
